Smart. Simple. Flexible.

Flexio is the open source solution to internal communication in companies.

Starting with the idea that the email communication is becoming broken as we live in an age where technology is getting stronger every day, Flexio is created to remove every problem that leads to missed meetings, misunderstandings and bad communication in general. Flexio's features for creating notifications, posts, updating each user's info and much more is making it the best solution to every day problems at work. And best of all, it's totally free! However, if you'd like to support the sole developer who made Flexio, you are free to do so. The Donate option is always here.

Everyone can stay informed, productive, and motivated. The solution is here, because Flexio keeps it smart, simple, and flexible.
